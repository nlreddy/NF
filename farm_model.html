60x60 ft ZBNF Plot Overview

This model includes multi-layer cropping, border trees, composting zones, and water conservation techniques.

×

Multi-Layer Cropping

Combines tall crops (e.g., banana), medium crops (e.g., papaya), creepers (e.g., beans), and ground cover (e.g., turmeric) to maximize space and biodiversity.

×

Border Trees & Windbreaks

Native trees like Gliricidia and Neem provide shade, wind protection, and biomass for mulching and composting.

×

Composting & Jeevamrut

On-site compost pits and Jeevamrut preparation zones enrich soil with microbial life, using cow dung, urine, jaggery, and pulse flour.
