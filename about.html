Our Story

Nirmal Prakrutik Krishi was born from a passion for sustainable agriculture & living and a deep respect for nature. We follow traditional farming methods that enrich the soil and produce healthy, chemical-free crops.

Located in the heart of Telangana, our farm is a haven for biodiversity and community-driven agriculture.
